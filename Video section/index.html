AI is future - Introduction to AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the world by enabling machines to learn from data, make decisions, and perform tasks that traditionally required human intelligence.... From self-driving cars and voice assistants to advanced medical diagnostics and predictive analytics, AI is revolutionizing industries and enhancing our daily lives. Its capabilities in natural language processing, image recognition, and automation are opening new frontiers of innovation and efficiency. As AI technology continues to evolve, it holds the promise of solving complex problems, improving productivity, and creating opportunities for a smarter, more connected future.
